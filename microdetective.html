    • The Characters

    Character Description

    Jasmine

    Title: Junior Research Fellow

    Jasmine is under time pressure with her research projects. She often skips her lunch and keeps some chocolate in the cold room.

    Mrs. Sophia

    Title: Senior Lab Manager

    Mrs. Sophia arranges the re-certification for the safety cabinets in the lab.

    Dr. Ethan

    Title: Assistant Professor

    Dr. Ethan is the department's fire safety coordinator. He arranges fire drills and checks the conditions of all firefighting equipment in the department regularly.

    Jackson

    Title: Year 1 Undergraduate Student

    Jackson accidentally spilled some hydrochloric acid on the floor. He is afraid of being scolded by Prof. Garang. He cleaned up the spills with a mop but did not inform anyone.

    Isabella

    Title: Phd Student

    Isabella comes to the lab to run a gel electrophoresis before meeting her friends for dinner. She arrives at the lab wearing high heels, but she puts on her lab coat and safety glasses while conducting her experiment.

    Prof. Garang

    Title: Associate Professor

    Prof. Garang always takes action to correct unsafe practices and working conditions in the lab. Larry will put on his personal protective equipment (PPE) if he sees Prof. Garang in the lab.

    Larry

    Title: Phd Student

    Larry does not like to wear the lab coat and safety glassess when working in the lab.

    Mia

    Title: Senior Research Fellow

    Mia conducts risk assessment before carrying out experiments with new procedures.

    Noah

    Title: Laboratory Technologist

    When no one watching, Noah pours the TAE buffer contaminated with ethidium bromide down the sink.

    Kid

    Title: Year 4 Undergraduate Student

    Kid keeps the cap of the bottle tightly when microwaving the nutrient agar as he is afraid of contamination.

    Charlotte

    Title: Research Assistant

    Charlotte cannot find tongs to clear the broken beaker. She puts on cryo gloves and picks up the broken glass with her hands.

    Grace

    Title: Internship Student

    Grace prepares magnesium chloride with ethanol and sterilize it using autoclave.
